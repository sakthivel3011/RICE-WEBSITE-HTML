About Our Company

Established in January 2015, Sri Annakamatchi Traders is committed to delivering high-quality rice at the best value. We source paddy directly from farmers in Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, and Andhra Pradesh, ensuring freshness and quality in every grain.

Founded by Mr. P.T. Singaravel B.Com, we prioritize quality, fair pricing, and complete customer satisfaction in every transaction.
